About Getting Over It 

Getting Over It is a challenging physics-based game where you control a man named Diogenes, stuck in a cauldron, using only a hammer to climb an impossible mountain. The game tests your patience, skill, and determination, as even the smallest mistake can send you back to earlier stages. It’s a unique blend of frustration and satisfaction, rewarding persistence with each small victory.

How to Play Getting Over It

  • Control the Hammer: Use the mouse to move the hammer and swing it around. The hammer’s motion is what allows Diogenes to climb and navigate obstacles.
  • Climb Obstacles: Swing your hammer to grab and pull yourself over ledges, rocks, and other obstacles. The movement requires precision and timing, so every swing counts.
  • Avoid Falling: Falling from high places sends you back down the mountain, often causing frustration. Keep your balance and use the hammer carefully to avoid setbacks.
  • Patience is Key: The game’s difficulty is intentional. Take your time, stay calm, and learn from your mistakes. Perseverance is the secret to success in "Getting Over It."

Every attempt is part of the experience, offering a mixture of challenge and triumph.
